About Me

Hafa adai, my name is Jason Arjona. I was born and raised on a little island called Guam. In Guam, hafa adai means hello in the Chamorro language. I recently moved to the mainland in 2021 and currently reside in Seattle. I moved here to try and break into the tech industry so now I am a student in the Year Up program for quality assurance. I eventually want to work my way up to become a game developer. There are multiple video game companies that I would love to work for but Bethesda would be my number one choice. I really enjoyed playing their video games growing up. I invested so much time into their games Skyrim and Fallout. Aside from wanting to be a video game developer, a goal of mine is to be able to create a software that lets blind people play any type of video game through haptic feedback.
